Welcome to the Mithril Forge manual. Mithril Forge is a browser-based encounter tracker that runs across two screens: a private view for the Dungeon Master and a public view for the players.

Instead of writing initiative orders on a whiteboard or tracking hit points on scratch paper, you manage the fight from your laptop while your players watch the turn order update live on a TV.

### What it does

- **Dual-screen tracking:** You see stat blocks, exact HP, and hidden conditions. Players see only the turn order, names, and health status indicators.
- **Built-in monster stats:** You can search the SRD database to pull in standard monsters with their full stat blocks, Armor Class, and hit points.
- **Fast combat management:** You can roll initiative for an entire group of goblins at once, apply area-of-effect damage to multiple targets, and track temporary hit points.
